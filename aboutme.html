Dr. Richard Scott Rhodes (License PSY9902)

Richard Scott Rhodes, Psy.D. is California licensed clinical psychologist – (PSY9902). Richard has over thirty years experience as a therapist in San Diego helping couples and individuals achieve higher self-esteem, stronger relationships, more effective problem solving and a more robust and joyful life.

Dr. Rhodes graduated from San Diego State University with a BA in Social Science. He earned a Masters in Marriage and Family Therapy and a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology from United States International University. He has served in the US Navy and was a Firefighter before entering Graduate School.

Dr. Rhodes brings his experience with the military service and being a firefighter into his treatment of PTSD and trauma. He also treats ADHD. Adults and teens with ADD and ADHD.

Dr. Rhodes’ therapeutic style is compassionate, and insightful; invoking practical solutions. He combines Cognitive-Behavioral and supportive therapies to help with clarity and thoughtful decision making. Dr. Rhodes helps you to deal with stress, guiding you to clearly understand yourself, assists in seeing options and dealing with your circumstances. He helps resolve difficulties; freeing you to live a happier, richer and more abundant life.
